CSPIT

drashti Chandubhai S. Patel Institute of Technology (CSPIT) is one of the premier engineering institutes of CHARUSAT University. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. The institute focuses on academic excellence, innovation, research, and practical learning. With modern laboratories, experienced faculty members, and strong industry connections, CSPIT prepares students for successful careers in engineering and technology.

DEPSTAR

Devang Patel Institute of Advance Technology and Research (DEPSTAR) is a renowned institute of CHARUSAT University dedicated to advanced education in engineering and technology. It offers programs in fields such as Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and other emerging technologies. The institute emphasizes research, innovation, and skill development to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving technological world. DEPSTAR provides students with excellent learning resources, modern infrastructure, and opportunities to work on real-world projects.

RPCP

Ramanbhai Patel College of Pharmacy (RPCP) is one of the leading pharmacy colleges under CHARUSAT University. The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in pharmacy. It is committed to providing quality education, promoting research, and developing competent healthcare professionals. RPCP features advanced laboratories, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on practical training. Through academic excellence and research activities, the institute prepares students to contribute effectively to the pharmaceutical and healthcare sectors.
