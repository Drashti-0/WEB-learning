CSPIT

[CSPIT] Chandubhai S. Patel Institute of Technology (CSPIT) is one of the premier engineering institutes of CHARUSAT University. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical Engineering. The institute focuses on academic excellence, innovation, research, and practical learning. With modern laboratories, experienced faculty members, and strong industry connections, CSPIT prepares students for successful careers in engineering and technology.
